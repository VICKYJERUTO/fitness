ABOUT OUR FITNESS

A fitness app specially designed for college going students who find it very difficult to manage a fix workout timetable and diet with their daily college routine.
To provide students with a guide that provides them with a flexible workout and diet plan to meet their respective fitness goals in mass gain fat loss shredding that would be possible to be followed parallelly with their day to day college schedule.

Unlike other fitness apps that are designed keeping in mind the major crowd of users that is the office going crowd and elderly people this app is specially designed for teenagers and college going students. It’ll be specially designed keeping in mind a normal student’s daily schedule, diet,budget and source availbilities
